I/O Wikipedia翻译文档

Posted on 2016-07-23   |     |   visitors

Wikepedia对I/O的中文文档解释只有一句话，因此我对应地翻译了英文文档。

中文文档：

I/O（英语：Input/Output），即输入/输出，通常指数据在内部存储器和外部存储器或其他周边设备之间的输入和输出。

英文文档：

In computing, input/output or I/O (or, informally, io or IO) is the communication between an information processing system, such as a computer, and the outside world, possibly a human or another information processing system.

– 在计算机中，输入/输出或者I/O（非正式叫法,io或IO）是信息处理系统间的互交，比如计算机与人或者其他信息处理系统等之间的交流。

Inputs are the signals or data received by the system and outputs are the signals or data sent from it.

– 输入是系统接收到的信号或者数据，而输出是由系统发出的信号或者数据。

The term can also be used as part of an action; to “perform I/O” is to perform an input or output operation.

– 该术语同样可以被当做一种行为，去“执行 I/O”等于去执行一个输入或输出操作。

I/O devices are used by a human (or other system) to communicate with a computer. For instance, a keyboard or mouse is an input device for a computer, while monitors and printers are output devices.

– I/O设备被人（或者其他系统）用来与计算机进行沟通。举个例子，一个键盘或者鼠标是一个计算机的输入设备，而显示器和打印机是输出设备。

Devices for communication between computers, such as modems and network cards, typically perform both input and output operations.

– 计算机间用来互交的设备，如调制解调器和网卡，主要用于输入和输出操作。

Note that the designation of a device as either input or output depends on perspective.

– 值得注意的是，不管输入还是输出的设备的设计取决于不同的视角。

Mouse and keyboards take physical movements that the human user outputs and convert them into input signals that a computer can understand;

– 鼠标和键盘用于用户输出的具体行为，并将它们转换成能让计算机编译的输入信号。

the output from these devices is the computer’s input.

– 从这些设备（鼠标和键盘）的输出是计算机的输入。

Similarly, printers and monitors take signals that a computer outputs as input, and they convert these signals into a representation that human users can understand.

– 同样的，打印机和显示器将计算机的输出信号作为输入，同时将它们转换为用户可以理解的形式。

From the human user’s perspective, the process of reading or seeing these representations is receiving input; this type of interaction between computers and humans is studied in the field of human–computer interaction.

– 从用户的视角看，读取这些形式的过程是接受（来自设备）输入，这种人与计算机之间交互的形式被“人机交互学”领域所研究。

In computer architecture, the combination of the CPU and main memory, to which the CPU can read or write directly using individual instructions, is considered the brain of a computer.

– 在计算机体系结构中，中央处理器与主存储器的结合，被视为计算机的大脑，可以直接使用个人指令进行读写。

Any transfer of information to or from the CPU/memory combo, for example by reading data from a disk drive, is considered I/O.

– 任何信息传输到中央处理器/内存，或由其传来的信息，被视为I/O，例如读取磁盘驱动器的数据。

The CPU and its supporting circuitry may provide memory-mapped I/O that is used in low-level computer programming, such as in the implementation of device drivers, or may provide access to I/O channels.

– 中央处理器和其配套的电路系统，或可能提供用于低级别计算机程序的存储器映射输入/输出。如运行磁盘驱动器时，或可能会提供I/O通道。

An I/O algorithm is one designed to exploit locality and perform efficiently when exchanging data with a secondary storage device, such as a disk drive.

– I/O算法旨在当与二级存储交换数据时，进行本地地开发和有效地操作，如磁盘驱动器。
